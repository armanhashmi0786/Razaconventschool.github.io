About Our School

Raza Convent School is a nurturing English-medium institution dedicated to providing quality education from Nursery to Class 5. Our focus lies not only in academic excellence but also in nurturing strong language skills in English, Urdu, and Arabic, enabling our students to become confident communicators and culturally aware individuals.

✅ A well-stocked free library to inspire a love for reading ✅ CCTV surveillance for safety and security ✅ Clean drinking water and hygienic facilities ✅ Computer education to build digital literacy from an early age ✅ Comfortable and safe bus transportation

At Raza Convent School, we aim to build a strong foundation for every child in a caring, disciplined, and inspiring environment.
